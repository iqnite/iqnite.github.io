IQBit >> Eggsplode >> Privacy Policy

By using Eggsplode, you agree to the privacy policy:

What we store

The only kinds of data Eggsplode stores are user IDs, channel IDs, message IDs, and interaction IDs of players who have interacted with the app.

What we do not store

Eggsplode does not store any private information. Data from Discord profiles might be temporarily stored for visualization purposes.

How we use your data

User data is solely used to make the game function. We might use it for debugging purposes when you report a bug.

What we share

We do not share any of the user data we store with third parties.

Which permissions we have

Eggsplode does not use any additional Discord app intents. It has no access to messages and cannot get information about users who did not interact with it.

Data deletion

To request the deletion of your persistent data, please contact us through our website.
