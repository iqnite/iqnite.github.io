By using Eggsplode, you agree to the privacy policy.

What we store

Eggsplode stores minimal user data to ensure the app functions correctly. This includes user, channel, server, and interaction names, descriptions, and IDs. No additional information is stored without your explicit consent. Eggsplode does not store any private information, including profile pictures, email addresses, phone numbers, friends, or messages.

How we use your data

The data collected by Eggsplode is solely to make the app function correctly and to improve your experience. This may include analyzing usage patterns and identifying bugs. We do not use your data to target advertisements.

What we share

We do not share any of the user data we collect with third parties.

Which permissions we have

Eggsplode does not use any additional Discord app intents. It has no access to messages and cannot get information about users who did not interact with it.

Data deletion

To request the deletion of your data, please contact us at iqnitetechnologies@gmail.com.
